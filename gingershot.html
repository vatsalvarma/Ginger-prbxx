What’s Special About This Drink?

The Ginger Shot is a powerful blend built around one of nature’s most respected roots. Ginger has been used for generations in traditional remedies for its warming and revitalizing qualities. Combined with lemon for freshness, honey for balance, and a hint of pepper for activation, this shot delivers a sharp yet refreshing kick.

Shake & Gulp Ginger Shot is designed for modern lifestyles that need quick, natural support. Since real ingredients settle, every bottle is meant to be shaken well before drinking.
